Ways to Donate to our Crowdfund Campaign

The best way to donate is to click on the "Donate Now" button here or at the top of this page, but we understand that not everyone wants to send money through PayPal.

If you would like to write us a check, or send your contribution in some other way, please email luz@konviv.com with the subject line "Donate to Konviv" and we will instruct you on how best to provide your support to Konviv.

*Your donation, unfortunately is not tax-deductible.


Terms of Service

I understand this is a non-tax deductible donation. I also understand that this is not an investment.


**We decided not to use GoFundMe, Kickstarter, or Indiegogo because of the excessive fees associated with crowdfunded donations on each platform.

“46% Nearly half of adults say they either could not cover an emergency expense costing $400.”

Sofia is a millennial living in Oakland. She works for a non-profit and loves what she does. However, because of high living costs every month she wonders if she is going to make it. Last month, Sofia’s car broke down, and repairing it cost her $700. Had Sofia had Konviv, she would have known exactly which buckets she could pull from and which ones she couldn’t to cover this expense. Because she didn’t have Konviv, when her car payment was charged, Sofia overdrafted, and was in the negative.

Thanks to You!

By donating, you will become part of the Konviv Family and journey. We'd be honored to have you.

If you donate and would like us to feature your name, headshot, amount, and/or reason for donating on our crowdfunding page and social media, we will gladly recognize your contribution. For donations above $500 we'll send you a Konviv shirt and some other goodies. For donations above $1000 you'll receive a surprise Konviv gift made just for you in the mail.

With your donation, you are not only supporting a couple of budding entrepreneurs, but also standing up for a world that is kinder, more inclusive, and empowering for all.

This is Ronnie. Ronnie was injured on the job and wasn’t able to work for 4 months. Like most Americans, Ronniie had little to no savings, and after his injury he had no idea how he was going to make ends meet. Had Ronnie had Konviv, he would have known exactly how long the little savings he had would last him, and when he would need to look for money from friends, a loan, or other means.

Other Ways to Help

There are many ways you can help the team as we get Konviv up and running. Do you know someone we should reach out to? Know someone that we should be in touch with about becoming an advisor or mentor? Know anyone that might be a phenomenal addition to team? Know institutional investors, angel investors, family funds we should reach out to? Any of these would be tremendously helpful.

Rather than donating, would you feel comfortable issuing Konviv a loan? That is another avenue we are actively pursuing. If this is something you would be interested in discussing with us please send us an email at luz@konviv.com and we'll be in touch about setting up a time to chat.

One more thing. We would be humbled if you could share this crowdfunding effort with all your friends and family. Konviv is a movement, and is about all of us coming together to stand for something larger than ourselves. We hope you'll join us.

Have some other creative idea to help out? Do you have questions you want to ask us? Please let us know!
